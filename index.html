Care Taker


Care Taker is a machine learning model, which basically takes care of the moisture prediction of your plant, farm or any other perticular field you are using. In Care taker currently we have used Multinomial Linear Regression which predict the water content in soil based on the paramters passed, these parameters are temperature, humidity and time(Day).

Data Analyser


Data Analyser is data analytic model, which
analyses your soil content and lets you know the
NPK contents and nature of your soil.

The N, P, and K value of the sample are determined by absorption
light of each nutrient. The optical transducer is implemented as
detection sensor which consists of three LEDs aslight source and a
photodiode as a light detector. The wavelength of LEDs is chosen to fit
the absorption band of each nutrient.

By clicking on the "Lets analyser" button you can try our Care Taker model

About

Plant Keeper

Product 1:

Plant keeper 1 is device specially modified on such a way that it take cares of you plant automatically by watering your plants whenever needed and also send you it's update , such as has there been any movement around your plants (for animals ), moisture level , surrounding temperature, humidity , as well as weather condition directly into your smart phone , Yes you can access data about your plant from anywhere around the world .

Product 2:

Plant keeper 2 is device which is a better version of plant keeper 1, which is used in larger scale. It also works on manual mode, where it can water the lanes in farm according to there moisture content and also informs the data in blynk console. This model will be used for greater data collecting from differnt regions from farms and local area. These data sets can be used to train the Machine learning model, which on further predict the upcoming calamaties and can take proper precotions.

Future

Additional features

Plant keeper can be modified in many ways as agriculture is not a minor sector to work on. What we can do is as detection sensor which detect the Nitrogen, Phosphorus and Potassium contents in soil and upgrades the farmer on user interface, and the farmer can study his field more accurately and use right fertilizers.
Currently we are rellying on blynk console for user interface, retrieving datasets and Arduino uno for microcontrolling. With provided funds we can create our user-interface and can produce cheaper microcontrollers.

Grounds

The grounds on which we are working are multiple, one may stand as water irrigation system automatically makes the water supply all over the field wherever necessary. And hence reduces the major work of farmers such checking the soil moisture regularly on time to time and deciding where and which lane should be provided with the water supply. This application and also be used provide accurate future data on water levels in water reserviours, on basis on these data and machine learning model we can predict the water levels in dams and huge canals and maintain the water levels so that in the time of heavy rains and more water flow, sudden flowing of water may cause flodding in near areas.
